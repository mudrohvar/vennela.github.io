My Approach

How I Build Products

Solve with empathy, scale with impact.

I approach product management by deeply understanding the people who use the product—whether they're learners, advisors, or global teams. Every feature, workflow, or system I design starts with the question: How will this make someone's work or life better?

Think big, start smart.

From AI-powered learning platforms to enterprise-scale financial tools, I balance ambition with execution. I love breaking down complex problems into intuitive, actionable solutions while keeping an eye on scalability and business impact.

Lead with collaboration.

Products succeed when teams succeed. I partner across engineering, design, content, and business teams to ensure every voice is heard, every detail considered, and every launch leaves a measurable impact.

Innovate fearlessly.

I'm constantly experimenting—whether it's AI-driven certification systems, automated translations, or new ways to streamline onboarding. Every experiment informs smarter, faster, more creative product decisions.

Career Path

My Journey

2022 - Present

Google

Product Manager

Building global learning systems at scale

At one of the world's most innovative ecosystems, I lead global learning systems that empower teams and partners across continents. My work blends product strategy, AI experimentation, and operational excellence to reimagine how people learn and grow at scale.

2021 - 2022

EY (Ernst & Young)

Product Consultant

Transforming enterprises into product-driven organizations

At EY, I had the opportunity to advise global enterprises as they evolved from project-based operations to true product-driven organizations. I designed playbooks, activation frameworks, and product strategies that helped Fortune 500 companies modernize how they build and scale. More than consulting, it was about enabling cultural change — turning processes into mindsets. This role refined my ability to lead through influence and structure ambiguity into action.

2018 - 2021

RobustWealth

Product Manager

Redefining what's possible in fintech

Joining a fast-scaling fintech startup taught me how to build from the ground up — translating vision into products that reshape how people invest. The solutions I helped design became the foundation for Principal Financial Group's D2C product which was adopted on acquisition. It reinforced a core belief: great products don't just solve problems — they redefine what's possible for an entire industry.

2014 - 2015

Amazon

Product Manager

Where I learned to design at scale

Amazon was where I learned the power of data, precision, and process. In one of the most metrics-driven environments in the world, I helped optimize systems that shaped global customer experiences. It was my introduction to designing at scale — where every insight, every improvement, affects millions. That foundation continues to guide me: move fast, stay curious, and always design with the end user in mind.
